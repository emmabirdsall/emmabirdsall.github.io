There are only two kinds of people in the world: the Irish, and those who wish they were.

We firmly believe that it's utterly impossible to be unhappy at an Irish pub.* With a pint of Guinness in your hand, Irish music in your ears, and comfort food in your belly, all the weights of the world will melt away. Our goal is to transport you from Covington, KY to the wonderful country of Ireland. It holds a near and dear place in our heart and we hope that we've done it justice. Drop in for food, fun and fellowship. Spend your weekend nights listening to live music and making new friends. If you're a regular, welcome back. If you've never been before, we look forward to meeting you. Sláinte!

*We cannot guarantee your happiness if your Premier League team loses, but we will be more than happy to pour you another pint to help ease the pain.
